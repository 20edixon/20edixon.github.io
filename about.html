About Me

Hello! I'm Elliott, an A Level student from Kent, currently studying Edexcel Computer Science. I am fluent in a variety of programming languages such as HTML, CSS and Python, that I have used in my studies and in the creation of this website.

I have a passion for coding and technology, and I am always looking for new ways to improve my existing skills and learn new ones.

In my free time, I enjoy working on personal coding projects, gaming, and playing a variety of musical instruments. History, particularly 15th Century Europe, is another personal intresest, one that I have been fortunate enough to reaserch as part of my studies.


I am also involved in Vex Robotics, and am the lead programmer for my school's team, though I take on a variety of other responsibilities, such as editing our notebook. As a team, we have accomplished much over the years, competing at national level, and even at international level in the 2022-2023 season.

This website serves as a portfolio of my work and a platform to share my thoughts and ideas on various topics related to technology and coding.

Feel free to explore the different sections of the website to learn more about me and my projects. If you have any questions or would like to get in touch, please don't hesitate to contact me through the contact page.
