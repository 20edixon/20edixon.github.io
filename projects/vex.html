2022-2023 Season - slapshot

Our team debuted in the 2022-2023 Season "Slapshot under the name AAA, standing for always above avarage. This season was by far our most sucessful, our robot, pictured on the left, was undefeated in the regional competitoins, and we finished as sixth best team in the country at the national level. We also won an award for the best constructed robot, and this qualified us for the world championship in Dallas, Teaxs. Here, we fianlly met out match, and finished 36th in our league.

The legacy of this robot is still very much alive however, even after its dismantleing. This piture of our robot is one of the first images that appears when searching "vex robotics" into google.

During the season I was one of the drivers for the team, though I was also involved in the construction and coding of the robot.
